What is the difference between NOA-OLSR and OOLSR ?
As of 28 April 2005, NOA-OLSR was based on the version of OOLSR released (0.99.15) ; hence NOA-OLSR has all the features of OOLSR. However NOA-OLSR doesn't support multiple interfaces (yet), and doesn't integrate the (unreleased) latest changes. In CVS terms, NOA-OLSR is a branch of OOLSR which is merged regularily.
